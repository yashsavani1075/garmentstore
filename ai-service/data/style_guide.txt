Wedding outfit guide:
For weddings and marriage functions, silk fabric is recommended.
Recommended wedding wear includes saree, lehenga choli, salwar suit, gown, sherwani, kurta pajama, and formal suit.
Good wedding colors include red, maroon, gold, royal blue, emerald green, cream, and beige.

Festival outfit guide:
For festivals, silk and cotton ethnic wear are recommended.
Recommended festival wear includes kurti, saree, salwar suit, kurta pajama, and lehenga choli.

Office outfit guide:
For office and formal wear, linen and cotton are recommended.
Recommended office wear includes shirts, trousers, formal pants, kurti, tops, and simple salwar suits.

Summer outfit guide:
For summer and hot weather, cotton and linen are recommended.
Recommended summer wear includes cotton shirts, t-shirts, kurtis, tops, and loose-fit clothes.
