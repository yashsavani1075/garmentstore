Return Policy:
- Returns are accepted within 7 days from the date of delivery.
- Products must be unused, unworn, unwashed, and undamaged.
- Original tags, labels, and packaging must be intact.
- Return shipping charges apply as per the return reason.
- Final sale items, custom orders, and intimate apparel are non-returnable.
- For returns, please contact our customer support with your order details.

Shipping Policy:
- Delivery within India takes 3-7 business days depending on location.
- International shipping takes 7-15 business days.
- Shipping charges are calculated at checkout based on weight and destination.
- Orders are processed within 24-48 hours.
- You will receive a tracking number once your order is shipped.
- We partner with reliable courier services for timely delivery.

Payment:
- We accept online payments via Credit Cards, Debit Cards, Net Banking, and UPI.
- Cash on Delivery (COD) is available for select locations.
- All payments are processed through secure payment gateways.
- Prices are inclusive of applicable taxes.
- We do not store any sensitive payment information on our servers.
